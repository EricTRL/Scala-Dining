Transfer money

As an association board member you can transfer money from your association account to user accounts or other associations. Use this to allow members of any association to deposit money in their account. Details on how this works can be found on the help page.

A money transfer can only be undone by the receiving party! Check the fields carefully before creating a new transaction.
