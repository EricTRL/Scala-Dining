Transfer money

You can transfer money from your own balance to other users or an association. Your balance cannot become negative. You can use this to withdraw money from your account, to do this search a board member (preferably treasurer) from your association and ask for help.

A money transfer can only be undone by the receiving party! Check the fields carefully before creating a new transaction. To undo a transfer, ask the receiving party to create a new opposite transaction.
